Master the Martial Arts!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four techniques below. By clicking on a technique you will add a specific amount of damage to your total combo

You win the game by matching your total combo to the random number, you lose the game if your total combo goes above the random number.

The value of each technique is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each technique.
